About Digital Design

This course introduces the fundamentals of 2D and video design through digital media. Starting with the fundamentals of graphic design and production, and through application and hands-on experience, students will be able to understand the science behind having an artistic eye. Students will then be able to combine their gained knowledge into a final project and leave with a self-made product.

Num of students: 15

Total duration: 5 weeks

Material: Python fundamentals, Projects in python, Duration: 2 weeks

Practical experience: Real world projects from Liorra/Brightmind/Zaio, Duration: 3 weeks
