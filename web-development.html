About Web Development

This course provides students a holistic overview of engineering management consulting principles and a high-level technical review of the field of environmental engineering, with applicable design questions and case studies. In this course, students will learn that knowledge and application of management consulting principles can provide a solution to a client’s problem in an efficient and systematic way that is within the scope of work, adheres to a followed schedule, and stays within budget. Additionally, in this course, environmental engineering topics such as air pollution, solid and hazardous waste, and water and wastewater will be discussed in technical detail. The rest of the course will consist of applying learned management consulting principles in conjunction with real world example projects from the water and wastewater consulting industry. Students will walk away from this course with a strong foundation in management consulting and environmental engineering.

Num of students: 15

Total duration: 5 weeks

Material: HTML, CSS, Javascript, React & Firebase
